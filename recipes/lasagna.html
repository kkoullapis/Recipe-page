Lasagna

Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • lasagna noodles
  • red sauce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Cook beef
  3. Layer beef, cheese and pasta
  4. Bake for 35 minutes at 375 degrees

This is lasagna and it tastes amazing. I personally call it pizza cake

Some people may think otherwise.
